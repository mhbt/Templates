How it Works

  • Mount the enclosure and connect power.

  • Connect up to 4 cameras to the enclosure with ethernet cable.

  • Contact AlarmTransfer to activate the system and setup recording schedule.

  • Determine when and where real-time alert clips are sent - to user or responders.

  • Cameras detect people and/or vehicle activity and video alters are instantly sent via email and/or smartphone.

  • View live, event and 24/7 recorded video on the AlarmTransfer cloud video portal at anytime, from anywhere.

  • View live and play back video from an iOS or AndroidTM device.

  • Contact AlarmTransfer to quickly pull activity reports, or teach on your own.
