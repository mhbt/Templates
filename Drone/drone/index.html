Introducing DR-1A

Next-Generation Physical Security Delivered Remotely

The DR-1A program is designed to reduce or replace physical security staff by adding Intelligent Unmanned Aerial Systems (I-UAS) to an existing security and surveillance system.

request a quote

How it Works

The DR-1A program is a managed service. Customers subscribe to the hardware and services from AlarmTransfer for a period of time.
We are responsible for all hardware support and maintenance and we supply FAA licensed pilots to oversee your site.
Customers who subscribe to our Virtual Guard and Managed Access Control services will be rewarded with a hands-off approach to their physical security program.
Our team handles all events until they are escalated to a point of contact in your company, or law enforcement.

Better Than a Human Guards?

There are some things people just can’t do - like loiter hundreds of feet over a site for 5 hours with advanced thermal cameras to detect intruders in pitch black conditions. The fact is, some security guards have a hard time staying awake for 5 hours. After all, they’re only human. Think of the security gaps that humans create. What if you could fill those gaps and cut your costs at the same time?

Safety Through Advanced Technology

Keep employees and contractors safe. The DR-1A drone can carry an arsenal of high-tech sensors. Our drones can be scheduled for routine autonomous flights to monitor your facilities, or dispatched automatically by alarm events. Once in flight, our OPS Center can take control and fly manually if a threat needs to be monitored until law enforcement arrives. Our drones have Artificial Intelligence which provides image recognition with human and animal distinction. This allows drones to autonomously follow suspicious humans or vehicles. Live video can easily be shared with first responders to provide situational awareness.

Every Event is Verified

Positioned in strategic locations around your site, our system launches autonomous drone patrols and investigates alarms and incidents triggered by other sensors like surveillance cameras.

Save Money - Stop Responding to False Alarms

Let the drone do the work. When a sensor on your system is triggered, the drone will fly to the location of that sensor and investigate, sending a live HD video stream back to the command center.

Weatherproof Nest

Each drone docs in a high-tech enclosure that opens and closes, autonomously. The nest is armed with weather sensors to detect wind, precipitation, and even make intelligent flight decisions based on weather forecast.

Cost-Effective Scaling

The cost of the DR-1A program are fixed and designed to make the reduction of human guards something that can be done incrementally. Customers should expect to save 30% over a loaded security staff. Considering a single DR-1A drone has capabilities that exceed those of multiple humans for surveillance purposes, adding additional drones results in great cost of labor reductions.

The Swarm

The DR-1A program can be configured to operate as a “swarm.” That means multiple drones can be employed as a team, sharing information and collaborating in real-time. When an alarm is triggered on one side of your site a drone is dispatched. If another alarm is triggered during the same time, an additional drone can be dispatched to handle it. If a drone malfunctions, another will take its place and inherit its mission. If a drone investigates and the threat is verified, all drones can be immediately dispatched to overwhelm the intruder or monitor the threat.
